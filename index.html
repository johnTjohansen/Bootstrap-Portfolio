About Me

I am a business application programmer/analyst living in Los Angeles, CA working to expand my skills into web development. I grew up in a small town in the farm country of NW Illinois, near the Mississippi River, and moved to LA in 1983 after getting my AS degree in computer programming.

I love Big Band/Swing music, cooking, painting, playing piano, and camping. I play World of Warcraft. My favorite place is Yosemite. I'm inspired by the style of Tim Burton.
